Passion Pixel - An AI-Powered Career Guidance Platform

July 2025

Education

Problem Statement

Students and professionals face uncertainty in selecting the right career path. Traditional career counseling lacks personalized guidance for specific goals. Career decisions are made slowly due to a lack of real-time insights and data-driven support.

Solution Proposed

Passion Pixel is an AI-powered career counseling tool. This smart, conversational tool uses AI to guide users through career decisions via video and voice-based interactions, delivering personalized recommendations based on inputs & skill mapping.
  • AI-Powered Conversations
  • Behavioral Analysis
  • Real-Time Career Suggestions
